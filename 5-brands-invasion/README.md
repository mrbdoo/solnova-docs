## Interaction Mechanics

### 1. Portals (The Gateways)
A Portal is an interactive object that replaces a GIF on a planet.
*   **The Experience:** Clicking a portal leads to a mini-game (e.g., Space Race), a contest, or an instant reward.
*   **The Reward:** Users play to win **Artifacts** (Real Prizes like iPhones, MacBooks) or Digital Prizes (SNOVA, Skins).
*   **Hosting:** Brands can place Portals on their own stars OR **rent planets from players**.
    *   *If a Brand rents your planet, they pay you SNOVA.*
    *   *If a winning player finds a prize on your planet, you might get a cut.*

### 2. Nuggets (The Treasure Hunt)
No games, just pure exploration.
*   **The Campaign:** A Brand hides 10 iPhones among the millions of Satellites in the universe.
*   **The Hunt:** Users participating in the campaign will see one of their active Satellites randomly start **Glowing**.
*   **The Lottery:** Clicking a glowing Satellite is a lottery ticket.
    *   *Odds:* e.g., 10 Prizes / 1000 Participants = 1/100 chance.
*   **Double Win:** If a prize is found on a Satellite orbiting *your* planet, **BOTH** the finder and the planet owner win.
