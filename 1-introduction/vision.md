## User - User Interaction

**Sharing as a Core Mechanic**

**GIFs are the new Pokemon**
GIFs are rare. You can drop a Satellite only from the GIFs you have:
*   Ones uploaded by you to your Planets.
*   Ones dropped to your planet as a Satellite by others.
*   The GIFs of Planets YOU dropped satellites on.
*   **Gods of the Day:** First-time GODs of planets that became 100% bright.

You are collecting cool GIFs by leaving Satellites on Planets. At the same time, you act like a flower attracting Bees (other users) by showing up more cool and interesting GIFs.
